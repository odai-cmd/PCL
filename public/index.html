Our Mission: A Circular Future

We believe in a world without waste. NARDO was born from a desire to combat the global tire pollution problem by giving this incredibly resilient material a second life. Our mission is to create beautiful, functional furniture that contributes to a circular economy.

Each piece is not only a statement of style but a testament to sustainability. The inherent properties of vulcanized rubber make our furniture exceptionally durable, weather-resistant, and perfect for both indoor and outdoor spaces.

How It's Made

Our meticulous upcycling process ensures every product meets the highest standards of quality and design.

1

Collect & Select

We partner with recycling facilities to source end-of-life tires, carefully selecting them based on quality and structural integrity.

2

Clean & Craft

Each tire undergoes an intensive, eco-friendly cleaning process. Our artisans then cut, shape, and assemble the raw material.

3

Create & Finish

The piece is finished with sustainable materials and coatings, resulting in a unique, handcrafted piece of furniture ready for a new home.
